Once your account is registered and KYC is underway (or complete), take ten minutes to lock down your security settings. This is the most boring part of the process and also the most important.

- **Enable two-factor authentication (2FA)**: Use an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy) rather than SMS when possible. SMS-based 2FA is vulnerable to SIM-swapping attacks.

### How Maker and Taker Fees Work

If you're new to trading terminology, here's the quick version:

- **Maker fee** applies when you place a limit order that sits on the order book and gets filled later. You're "making" liquidity. Makers get lower fees because they help the market function.
- **Taker fee** applies when your order executes immediately against an existing order on the book. You're "taking" liquidity. Taker fees are slightly higher.

For a $50,000 monthly spot trading volume at Regular rates, you'd pay $40 if every trade was a maker order, or $50 if every trade was a taker order. At VIP 1 rates (0.06% maker / 0.08% taker), that same volume costs $30 or $40 respectively.
